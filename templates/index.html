Welcome to the Interactive Cryptography Visualization Application

About the Project

This web-based application is part of a Masters dissertation project in Advanced Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence. The goal is to enhance cryptography education through interactive visualizations of key cryptographic primitives and protocols.

Research Question

To what extent does this interactive visualization application enhance student understanding, engagement, and reduce mathematics anxiety with the selected cryptographic concepts compared to traditional teaching methods?

Application Features

  • Adheres to established visualization design principles and best practices for educational technologies
  • Provides step-by-step visualizations of the encryption and decryption processes
  • Allows user interaction to explore and understand the concepts
